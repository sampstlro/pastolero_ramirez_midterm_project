Hyundai

Hyundai Motor Company has served as the trailblazer of Korea’s automobile industry since rolling out its Pony, developed with its own exclusive technology. Hyundai Motor Company has risen as a globally recognized automobile manufacturer that exports its branded vehicles to over 200 countries. It is equipped with production bases all around the world. It is expanding the automobile market through its success in mass-producing hydrogen-powered vehicles for the first time in the world and launching GENESIS, a high-end brand, while propelling the future mobility industry forward based on its leadership in autonomous driving and connectivity technologies. It seeks to find better solutions for humanity by achieving technological innovation under the aim of "Progress for Humanity".

Why You Should Choose Hyundai

There are many reasons to purchase a new, certified or pre-owned Hyundai. First, Hyundai is charitable, community-oriented, and highly values its employee’s thoughts and opinions during the design and decision processes.. Hyundai vehicles are made by the people, for the people. They believe in charitable and environmentally friendly ways to market their cars. For instance, with the purchase of every new Hyundai, Hyundai donates to Hope on Wheels, which funds pediatric cancer research. Hyundai is also known for its many warranties as well as its Complimentary Maintenance Program, Hyundai Shopper Assurance and Hyundai Owner Assurance programs.

Warranty

Hyundai is known for including America’s Best Warranty standard on every new Hyundai. This gives you the added peace of mind and supports Hyundai’s commitment to provide vehicles of high quality, dependability, and reliability. With every new Hyundai, you get a 10-year/100k mile powertrain limited warranty, 5-year/60k mile new vehicle limited warranty, 7-year/unlimited mile anti-perforation warranty, 5-year/unlimited mile 24-hour roadside assistance, 12-month/12k mile replacement parts and accessories limited warranty, 8-year/80k mile federal emission defect and performance warranty and a 10-year/100k mile hybrid system component warranty.

Community

As a brand, Hyundai is extremely community oriented. They have created an organization that supports cancer research (Hyundai Hope On Wheels), they care about jobs (they design, engineer and build many of their cars right here in America), they care about their drivers (so they build technology and programs that always put you first), and they care about the future (they strive to build a greener and more accessible world for our children).

Safety

If you’re looking for safety in your vehicle, look no further than a Hyundai. As of 2/2021, Hyundai has 7 combined Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS) 2021 TP SAFETY PICK PLUS and TOP SAFETY PICK-rated vehicles within its lineup. The top award winning models are: Palisade (TSP+), Nexo (TSP+), Veloster with optional front crash prevention and specific headlights (TSP), Sonata with specific headlights (TSP), Kona with optional front crash prevention and specific headlights (TSP), Tucson with optional front crash prevention and specific headlights (TSP) and Venue with specific headlights (TSP)
