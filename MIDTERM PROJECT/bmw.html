BMW

BMW is one of the largest and most recognizable luxury car brands in the world. With millions of BMW vehicles on the road today, it’s almost impossible to drive around town without seeing a decent amount of their cars. As arguably one of the best-known German car manufacturers in the world, you may think you know everything about the luxury car company.

Why You Should Choose BMW

BMW was initially a manufacturer of airplane engines and a good one at that. The company set records in aviation for both altitude and speed, making key advancements in the industry before making the switch to the auto industry.

BUILD QUALITY

BMWs are known for their excellent build quality.Many other brands tend to cut corners on things like paintwork, interior materials, and dashboard design in order to save money. BMWs don’t do this – and won’t ever do this – they make sure everything has a premium look and feel, inside and out. This attention to detail is what sets BMW apart from the average car manufacturer. A new BMW may be costly, but consider it a worthy investment as you’ll be getting a superior product that will last for years.

SUPERIOR ELECTRONICS

The BMW Drive function is one of the best on the market, allowing you to always be ahead of the curve for in-car electronics. This system is designed to help drivers maintain control of their vehicles, even in adverse conditions. The BMW Drive function offers in-car communications and entertainment, as well as control of most secondary vehicle systems.

TOP PERFORMERS

Looking for speed and a smooth drive? BMW cars are known for their performance. They regularly place high on review charts, thanks to their powerful engines, superior horsepower, agility, and overall driving experience. No matter what class of vehicle it is, BMW tunes every model’s driving dynamics to perfection. From the base models to the performance variants, new BMWs outperform most of their competition when it comes to power, braking, handling, and driving satisfaction.
