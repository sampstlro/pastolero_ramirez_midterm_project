Nissan

Nissan Motor Co., Ltd., is Japan's second-largest automotive company, its headquarters are in Yokohama, Japan, and is part of the Renault-Nissan Alliance. Operating with approximately 236,000 employees globally, Nissan sold more than 4.9 million vehicles and generated revenue of 9.6 trillion yen (USD 116.16 billion) in fiscal 2012. Nissan delivers a comprehensive range of more than 60 models under the Nissan and Infiniti brands. In 2010, Nissan introduced the Nissan LEAF, and continues to lead in zero-emission mobility. The LEAF, the first mass-market, pure-electric vehicle launched globally, is now the best-selling EV in history.

Why You Should Choose Nissan.

The Nissan brand as a whole is synonymous with offering budget-friendly vehicles that are both visually appealing and functional. As a potential car buyer, you can’t ask for much more than that.

TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION.

Nissan strives to ensure that drivers making their way through Brentwood are backed by some of the newest technological vehicle upgrades in the industry. Ergonomics, design, and engineering serve as the foundational blueprints for any Nissan vehicle.

MAINTENANCE

Take care of your Nissan and your Nissan will definitely take care of you. Nissan vehicles as a whole are durably built, and will require minimal maintenance to continue running smoothly. And should you ever get your Nissan car, truck, or SUV maintained, you’ll be happy to hear that you’ll be able to save time, money, and energy by working with car specialists who are knowledgeable about Nissan vehicles.

FUEL ECONOMY

Nissan is sits among the industry leaders in providing fuel efficient vehicles for their drivers. Most of their cars can easily achieve a combined MPG of more than 20 miles-per-gallon, thus, saving you trips to the gas station. With better fuel economy, you’ll be able to travel further than ever before when driving through the streets of Murfreesboro.
