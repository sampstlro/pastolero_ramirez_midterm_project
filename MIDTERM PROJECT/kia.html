Kia

Kia Corporation was founded in May 1944 and is Korea’s oldest manufacturer of motor vehicles. From humble origins making bicycles and motorcycles, Kia has grown – as part of the dynamic, global Hyundai-Kia Automotive Group – to become the world’s fifth largest vehicle manufacturer.

Why You Should Choose Kia

Kia cars have a long standing name in the market and they provide a host of high-tech and innovative features. The Kia models not only look great but also have a high degree of user comfort and safety. These products also provide a great value for money.

Power-packed Performance

Kia cars give remarkable performance over long time. These cars are tested over time and give a satisfactory drive quality and work over long use without maintenance issues and breakdowns. Kia cars rank high on efficiency. In fact, its engineering team has put in a lot of effort to improve the mileage and reduce Carbon Dioxide emissions. These rank at par or better than average figures by leading manufacturers.

Dashing designs

Kia cars are designed to incorporate most features demanded by clients. The styling is not only functional but also appealing to view. In fact, the styling makes Kia more desirable than many older players.Kia cars look really good, and you will feel great driving its good models. Looks play an ever important role in selection by customers in these times.

Wonderful warranty:

Kia cars provide a warranty for seven years ever since they did first in 2007. Initially, it was available for select models, but now the entire range comes with this wonderful warranty. This creates a very good attraction for users and testifies the confidence the company has in its various models.
